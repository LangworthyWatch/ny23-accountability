February 20, 2026 · SCOTUS IEEPA Ruling

Supreme Court strikes down tariffs 6–3. Langworthy says they "proven effective." The numbers disagree.

Hours after the Court ruled IEEPA tariffs illegal, Langworthy praised them as leveling the playing field. Here's what the data actually shows.

False
Tariffs "leveled the playing field"
U.S. goods trade deficit hit all-time record $1.24 trillion in 2025. Manufacturing lost 103,000+ jobs since Jan 2025. NY Fed: 94% of tariff costs paid by Americans.
Misleading
Ruling was just about "IEEPA interpretation"
Technically narrow, but voided ~half of all U.S. customs duties. Created $160–175B refund liability. Effective tariff rate dropped from 17% to ~8% overnight.
Partial
Trump has "several legal tools" remaining
Section 122 invoked same day — but capped at 15% and expires in 150 days. Cannot replicate uncapped IEEPA tariffs up to 145% on China.
Partial
Western NY "hollowed out" by free trade
Pain is real: Dunkirk poverty 24.9%, Olean 24.1%. But Ball State: 88% of job losses from automation, not trade. Tariffs now hurting the district.
