The structural problem
Every dollar National Grid invests in infrastructure earns a guaranteed 9.5% return — paid by ratepayers through delivery charges. In H1 2025/26, NG replaced 159 miles of leak-prone pipe in New York alone. Executive bonuses are tied to financial metrics that reward rate increases — not affordability. Gov. Hochul's 2026 ratepayer protection proposal would tie executive pay to affordability metrics. Langworthy's Energy Choice Act could undermine that accountability.
