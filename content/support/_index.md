# Support This Work

NY-23 Accountability Tracker is an independent civic research project. It is not affiliated with any political party, campaign, or organization.

## What Your Support Covers

Your contribution helps with:

- **Web hosting and domain costs** — Keeping the site online and accessible
- **Source archiving** — Preserving sources via Internet Archive and Archive.today
- **Records access** — FOIA requests and database subscriptions
- **Research tools** — Access to congressional records and news archives

## All Documentation Remains Free

All fact-checks and documentation remain freely accessible regardless of donations. This project exists to serve the public interest.
