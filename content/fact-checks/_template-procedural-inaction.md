## How to Use This Template

1. **Research the bill:**
   - Find bill number on Congress.gov
   - Check committee referral date
   - Track any committee action (or lack thereof)

2. **Verify committee membership:**
   - Use congress-legislators repo to confirm Langworthy was on committee during relevant period
   - Document his role (subcommittee assignments matter too)

3. **Find public statements:**
   - Search Langworthy's press releases
   - Check Congressional Record for floor speeches
   - Look for local news interviews
   - Review social media posts

4. **Calculate the gap:**
   - Days between statement and inaction
   - Specificity of promise vs. vagueness of follow-through
   - Pattern: Does this repeat with other bills?

5. **Document impact:**
   - Use CBO reports for fiscal impact
   - Find district-specific data from Census/agencies
   - Quote constituent groups affected

6. **Cite everything:**
   - Link to primary sources
   - Archive URLs in case they disappear
   - Screenshot social media posts

7. **Keep neutral tone:**
   - Let the facts speak
   - Avoid editorial language
   - Present timeline objectively
   - Quote directly, don't paraphrase
