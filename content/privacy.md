## Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This helps us improve content and identify technical issues.

Google Analytics collects:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- General geographic location (country/region level)
- Browser and device type

Google Analytics does **not** collect:
- Your name or email address
- Your exact location
- Any information you submit through forms

## Form Submissions

When you submit correspondence through our forms, your information goes directly to Formspree, a third-party form processor. We use this information only to document constituent correspondence patterns. Personal details are redacted before any information is published.

## No Tracking Cookies for Advertising

We do not use advertising cookies or share data with advertisers.
