HIS CLAIM
Canada's "forest management failures" are a "policy choice," and there should be "consequences."

THE SCIENCE

→ Our smoke does the same. In 2020, US wildfire smoke covered 5+ Canadian provinces and shut down Vancouver's mail.
→ The US is having its own severe fire season right now.
→ On climate, he voted NO on clean energy and YES to repeal it. And the DOGE effort he co-founded a caucus around cut the Forest Service crews that run prescribed burns.
