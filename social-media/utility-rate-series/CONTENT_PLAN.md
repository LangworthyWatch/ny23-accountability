## Design System (NEW — bright social media variant)

- **Background**: #F5F7FA (soft gray-white — Instagram-friendly, not harsh white)
- **Card/Surface**: #FFFFFF
- **Primary text**: #1A202C (near-black)
- **Header/brand**: #1E3A5F (navy — matches site)
- **Accent blue**: #2B6CB0 (links/highlights)
- **Danger/alert**: #E53E3E (red for negative findings)
- **Positive**: #38A169 (green for comparison)
- **Gold/verdict**: #D69E2E (amber for verdicts/callouts)

- **Big numbers**: Impact, 120-160pt (the focal point of every graphic)
- **Headers**: Arial Bold, 42-48pt

## Posting Schedule (suggested)

Roll out 1-2 posts per day over ~10 days. Start with the concept, then the data, then accountability.

**Phase 1: Set the Frame (Days 1-2)**

| Day | Post | File | Purpose |
|-----|------|------|---------|
| Day 1 | Post 0: What Is Regulatory Capture? | `post00_regulatory_capture.png` | Teach the concept — the 6-step pipeline gives people a framework to understand every subsequent post |

| Day 2 (PM) | Post 2: The Breakdown | `post02_breakdown.png` | Back up the hook with the full bar chart |

**Phase 2: The Deep Dive (Days 3-6)**

| Day | Post | File | Purpose |

| Day 6 | Post 7: $75.7M Lobbying Machine | `post07_lobbying.png` | Follow the money — connects to Post 0's framework |

**Phase 3: Accountability (Days 7-9)**

| Day | Post | File | Purpose |
|-----|------|------|---------|
| Day 7 | Post 6: Complaints +84% | `post06_complaints.png` | Consequences — people are telling regulators |
| Day 8 | Post 8: 18.4% Silence | `post08_silence.png` | Direct accountability — Langworthy won't address it |

**Why this order works:**
- Post 0 (regulatory capture) teaches the concept cold — no data yet, just "here's how this works"
- Posts 1-2 deliver the headline finding and the evidence
- Posts 3-7 go deeper into each step of the pipeline from Post 0
- Posts 8-9 bring it back to Langworthy specifically
- Post 10 sends everyone to the full investigation

**Re-share strategy:** After Day 9, re-share Posts 0 and 1 to Stories with "In case you missed it" — these are the gateway posts.
