- Will expire after 2028 tax year unless extended
- No legislation introduced to make it permanent

**Verification Result:**
The fact-check is **accurate**. The provision is now law, and its limitations (temporary, income-limited, deduction not elimination) remain as described. The fact-check's critique of the "88% will no longer pay tax" language is validated.
